I.The Passes

Distances are round-trip to the pass. Most are day-hikeable without a permit; overnight needs a wilderness permit. The bottom four — Taboose, Baxter, Sawmill, Shepherd — start on the hot desert floor and gain ~6,000 ft. Treat them with respect: pre-dawn starts, extra water.

II.The 14ers

Reality check: only Whitney, Langley, and White Mountain are true hikes. Everything else is mountaineering (Class 2–4) with real exposure and rockfall — build up to these, and rope up on the Palisade spires.
